A Candle, a Classroom and Courage

Before LSEG: I was a teacher — and a fighter for stability

For years I worked as an English teacher in Ukraine. It wasn't just a job — it was part of who I was. I loved helping children grow, witnessing their small successes and knowing I had made a difference.

But when the war started everything changed. I kept working even in the dark — literally. Some days I taught online with only candlelight or a flashlight balancing courage and exhaustion.

Deep down I knew I needed safety. Stability. A future where I could focus on growing instead of just surviving.

In February 2023 I left Ukraine for Poland. I had no job, no network and no certainty. Just a quiet belief that this wasn't the end of my story — it was the beginning of a new one.

🍃

Starting From Zero

I didn't just move to Poland. I reinvented myself.

Arriving in Poland meant more than finding a new home — it meant becoming someone new.

I started from scratch. I explored every opportunity and in that search I remembered something from childhood: I had always imagined myself working in a big company contributing to something meaningful.

That childhood dream turned into action. I polished my CV and applied to LSEG.

It wasn't easy. I wasn't sure I had the right background but I was willing to learn.

That willingness became the key.

🍃

My First Day

November 4: A New Beginning at LSEG

I remember my first day clearly — the mix of excitement, nerves and curiosity.

There was so much to take in: a new environment, new systems, new expectations. I was surrounded by professionals and processes I had never seen before.

What stood out most was the culture: LSEG welcomed me with openness. It gave me space to ask, to listen, to grow.

I paid attention, took notes, signed up for courses and leaned on the experience of my colleagues. Slowly but surely I began to find my place.

🍃

Confidence in Progress

Six Months Later: Still Learning but No Longer Afraid

Today I work with bonds — something that once seemed far outside my world. I've built a foundation of knowledge I never imagined I'd have.

I'm part of LSEG's "Back to Uni" program where I keep strengthening my skills. I take every opportunity to grow — and I've learned that professional development isn't a checkbox. It's a mindset.

The most important change?

I no longer doubt if I belong here. I know I do.

🍃

Still Growing

What's Next?

I have big dreams and I'm not afraid of them anymore.

One day I'd love to grow into the world of data science but I know that will take time, effort and learning. I'm not in a rush. I'm building something real.

With every challenge I take on, every question I ask and every piece of knowledge I gain I'm investing in my future.

Growth isn't a destination. It's the path I'm choosing — every single day.
